How do I debug an HTTP 500 error in Spring Boot?

Asked 5 mins ago | 10 helpful | 4 answers

I'm having trouble figuring out what's causing an HTTP 500 error in my Spring Boot application. Can someone guide me on the best practices for debugging this issue?

Clara Ugwu – 7 helpful Accepted

To troubleshoot, check your logs for specific stack traces and ensure your controller methods handle exceptions properly.

Bryan Okafor – 4 helpful

Make sure to check the HttpServlet stack trace to find the exact cause of the error.

Ada Ebube – 3 helpful

Verify that your application-context.xml is configured correctly. Misconfigurations can lead to 500 errors.
